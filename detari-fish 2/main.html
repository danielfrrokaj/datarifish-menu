Our Story

For three generations, our family has been serving the freshest fish from the Albanian coast. Located in the heart of Durres, we carefully select our fish daily from local fishermen who share our passion for quality.

Our Fish

Our fish comes directly from the pristine waters of the Adriatic Sea. We work closely with local fishermen who use sustainable fishing practices, ensuring both quality and environmental responsibility.
