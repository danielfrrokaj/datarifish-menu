About Detarifish

A Tradition of Fresh Seafood Excellence Since 1995

Our Story

For three generations, our family has been dedicated to bringing the finest seafood from the Albanian coast to your table. What started as a small family restaurant in Durrës has grown into a beloved establishment known for its commitment to quality and tradition.

Every morning, we personally select the freshest catch from local fishermen who share our passion for quality. Our chefs then craft these premium ingredients into delectable dishes that combine traditional recipes with modern culinary techniques.

Fresh Daily Catch

Our seafood is sourced daily from local fishermen, ensuring the highest quality and freshness for our customers.
